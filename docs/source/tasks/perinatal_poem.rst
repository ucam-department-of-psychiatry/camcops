Sources
~~~~~~~

- Royal College of Psychiatrists (2018).
  FROM-Perinatal: Framework for Routine Outcome Measures in Perinatal
  Psychiatry. College Report CR216.
  https://www.rcpsych.ac.uk/docs/default-source/improving-care/better-mh-policy/college-reports/college-report-cr216.pdf

- https://www.rcpsych.ac.uk/improving-care/ccqi/quality-networks-accreditation/perinatal-community-teams/standards-and-publications

- Inpatient version:
  https://www.snapsurveys.com/wh/s.asp?k=153777774423.
  "The in-patient measure consists of

  - 2 questions on mental health at first contact and discharge from service
    using a 5-point Likert scale,
  - 12 questions on patient experience using a 4-point Likert scale and
  - 6 questions specific to the MBU that also use a 4-point Likert scale."

- Community version:
  https://www.snapsurveys.com/wh/s.asp?k=148369367862.

  - "The measure for the perinatal community team use does not include the
    last 6 [inpatient] unit-specific questions."

- Content extracted from online version in the absence of another source.
